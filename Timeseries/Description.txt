Learning outcomes:
1. Understanding the basic blocks of Time series models
2. Preprocessing of the data to apply time series models
3. Handling the missing values in Time series
4. Split the data into Train and Test
5. Building the following time series models
a. Simple moving Average
b. Weighted Moving Average
c. Exponential smoothing
d. ARIMA models
6. Model diagnostics and Model evaluations
Let us build time series models in using e-commerce data and the goal is to predict the price of a certain product for future time period using time series algorithms and evaluating each of the algorithms.
